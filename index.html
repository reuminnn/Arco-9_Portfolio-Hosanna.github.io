OWNER

The owner of Pawcafe, Mariana Arco, had decided to build this cafe for all dog and coffee lovers. It is built to support any kind of dogs and their food, to individuals with different tastes. The owner once had a pet dog, but dies a few years later. To this day, the owner had decided to build Pawcafe to still express her love to pet dogs and to see the happy faces of her customers whenever they see their pets enjoying. This can be a form of leisure time to the owners and for their pets.

Staff workers has also said how much love the owner of Pawcafe has expressed for her establishment. They have said that they were treated well by the owner and by the regular customers as well. They've said that the recipes the owner comes up with were all good for the customers and for their pets, saying that she understood the pets well.

Family have said that ever since Mariana Arco was a child, she have always liked dogs in particular. They are proud to see their daughter using her knowledge on dogs and spreading them to dog owners / lovers as well.

About PawCafe:
PawCafe isn't just your average cafe; it's a haven where dogs and their owners can connect over delectable treats and beverages. We pride ourselves on providing a welcoming environment where the wagging tails of furry friends are always encouraged. Our menu is carefully curated to cater to both human and canine tastes, ensuring that everyone leaves with a satisfied palate and a happy heart.
Pawcafe is a dog-friendly cafe where you can enjoy delicious treats and beverages in the company of your furry friends. Our menu offers a variety of snacks and beverages for both humans and dogs, ensuring a delightful experience for all. Whether you're looking for a relaxing afternoon with your pup or a social gathering with fellow dog lovers, Pawcafe is the perfect destination.

Facilities:
Indoor Seating: Relax in our cozy indoor seating area, complete with designated dog-friendly zones to ensure the comfort of all guests.
Outdoor Patio: Soak up the sunshine and enjoy al fresco dining with your pup on our spacious outdoor patio.
Dog Play Area: Let your furry friend burn off some energy in our dedicated dog play area, equipped with toys and obstacles for endless fun.
Water Stations: Hydration is key! Help yourself to our complimentary water stations, conveniently located throughout the cafe for both dogs and humans.
Doggy Restroom Facilities: We understand nature's call! Our clean and accessible doggy restroom facilities are available for your convenience.

Operating Hours:
Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM
